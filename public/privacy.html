Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

Gamma Tech Services ("we", "our", "us") operates the ProjectIT mobile application and web platform. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

When you use ProjectIT, we collect:

  • Account information: name, email address, and profile photo you provide during signup.
  • Project data: projects, tasks, parts, time entries, comments, and files you create within the app.
  • Device information: device type, operating system, and push notification tokens for delivering notifications.
  • Usage data: pages visited and features used, to improve the product.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the ProjectIT service.
  • To send push notifications and email alerts you have opted into.
  • To authenticate your identity and secure your account.
  • To improve the app based on aggregated usage patterns.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use Supabase for authentication and database services, and Railway for application hosting. All data is transmitted over HTTPS.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: authentication and database.
  • Railway: application hosting.
  • Apple Push Notification Service (APNs): push notifications on iOS.
  • HaloPSA: optional integration for syncing tickets (only if enabled by your organization).
  • QuoteIT: optional integration for receiving accepted quotes (only if enabled by your organization).

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your project data is only accessible to members of your organization within ProjectIT.

Camera and Photo Library

ProjectIT requests camera access to take photos for task attachments and project files. Photos are uploaded to your project and are not shared externally. Camera access is optional and can be denied without affecting core functionality.

Push Notifications

You may opt in to receive push notifications for task assignments, due dates, and project updates. You can disable notifications at any time through iOS Settings or within the app.

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will remove your personal data within 30 days.

Your Rights

You may request to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Delete your account and associated data.
  • Export your data.

Children's Privacy

ProjectIT is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
